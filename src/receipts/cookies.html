Chocolate Chip Cookies

Perfect chocolate chip cookies with chewy centers and crisp edges - a classic family recipe.

Total Time
35 minutes
Yield
24 cookies (12 servings)
Difficulty
Easy
Category
Desserts
Prep
15 min
Bake
20 min
Total
35 min
Classic Recipe Kid-Friendly Make-Ahead Freezer-Friendly Family Favorite

Servings Calculator

Recipe scales from 1-48 servings (2 cookies per serving)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 3/4  cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2  cup granulated sugar
  • 2  large eggs, room temperature
  • 2  tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4  cups all-purpose flour
  • 1  tsp baking soda
  • 1  tsp salt (enhances chocolate flavor)
  • 2  cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1

Prep oven and pans

Preheat oven to 375F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone mats. This prevents sticking and ensures even browning.

2

Mix wet ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined. Beat in eggs one at a time, then vanilla extract.

3

Combine dry ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. This ensures even distribution of leavening agents.

4

Make cookie dough

Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips with a wooden spoon or spatula.

5

Scoop and bake

Scoop 2-tablespoon portions onto prepared baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart. Bake 9-11 minutes until edges are golden brown but centers still look slightly underbaked.

6

Cool properly

Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes.
