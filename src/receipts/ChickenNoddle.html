Chicken Noodle Soup

A cozy, nourishing classic made with chicken, veggies, and egg noodles-perfect for cold days.

Total Time
45 minutes
Yield
8 cups (4-6 servings)
Difficulty
Easy
Category
Soup
Prep
10 min
Cook
35 min
Total
45 min
Classic Recipe Comfort Food Family Favorite One-Pot Meal Weeknight Friendly

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts or tenderloins
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 2 cups cut egg noodles
  • 1 chicken bouillon
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp onion & garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dill weed
  • 1/8 tsp rosemary, sage & basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

1

Cook & shred the chicken

Defrost if needed. Boil chicken in water until fully cooked. Drain, let cool slightly, then shred and set aside.

2

Saute the vegetables

In the same pot, heat 1 Tbsp olive oil. Add chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook, stirring, until slightly browned and fragrant (about 3-5 minutes).

3

Add broth, chicken & seasonings

Return shredded chicken to the pot. Add chicken broth (or water + bouillon), minced garlic, onion and garlic powder, Italian seasoning, oregano, dill, rosemary, sage, and basil. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and cook for about 10 minutes.

4

Simmer & cook noodles

Lower heat to a gentle simmer. Add egg noodles and cook until tender, about 7-10 minutes.

5

Finish & serve

Taste and adjust salt & pepper. Ladle into bowls and enjoy warm.
